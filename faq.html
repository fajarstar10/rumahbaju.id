How long can the flowers last?
Our fresh flowers will last up to 3-5 days in Jakarta’s humid and warm weather. Make sure to keep the flowers happy in a ventilated room and away from direct sunlight, so you may continue to enjoy their blooming beauty!
How do I care for the flowers? The flowers can be taken out from the wrapper after 1-2 days. Trim the stems by cutting the ends at a slant and put it into a vase filled with fresh water. Change the water regularly and display the flowers at a cool spot away from direct sunlight. To prevent contamination, do make sure to remove any fading petals, leaves or stems from the water.
